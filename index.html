Certification

Al Tallah Drinking Water Supply is an HACCP Certified Company. Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points (HACCP) is a systematic preventive approach to food safety that addresses physical, chemical and biological hazards as a means of prevention rather than finished product inspection.

Water Quality and Purity

The company applies state-of-the-art technology to produce highly purified water that is safe, clean and delicious. The company has adopted some of the most stringent regulations in the country in order to protect our customers and the bottled water industry from unethical bottlers. By law, we test our water daily, weekly and annually. Purified water processes are regularly monitored for compliance with stringent chemical and microbiological standards.
