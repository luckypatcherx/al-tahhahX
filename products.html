Dispensers

These 4 litres cooling tanks are developed using ABS body and stainless steel tank as per international norms and guidelines. All our fast cooling tanks are suitable to be used for homes, institutions & offices. They are durable, reliable and have excellent performance.
Each bottle comes with a no-spill cap that ensures clean and uncontaminated water at all times. The no spill cap is never removed or opened until it is placed onto the dispenser. This helps to prevent unnecessary spillage and is a unique and hygienic way of handling your drinking water.
